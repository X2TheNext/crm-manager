The Concept

What is the U Street Passport?

A one-day scavenger hunt anchored at Cookies DC. Players walk the corridor, collect stamps at each stop with a secret word from your staff, and compete on a live leaderboard for prizes.

📲

Players Register

Phone number only. No app download. Passport lives in the browser — your stop is visible on the map before they leave home.

📍

They Walk to You

Your address is a checkpoint. The hunt sends players directly to your door with a reason to come in.

🗝️

Staff Share a Word

One secret word. Staff share it when a hunter walks in. Player enters it, earns the stamp. Zero setup.

🎁

They Redeem Your Perk

You set the offer. Free item, discount, priority seating — real conversions, not just impressions.

🍪

Anchored by Cookies DC

July 11 is Cookies DC's one-year anniversary on U Street. Every sponsor is co-branded with one of the country's most recognized cannabis lifestyle brands — on the passport, on the map, and in every promotional push.

Questions

Straight answers.

Do I need to change how I operate?

No. One staff member knows the secret word. When a hunter asks for it, they share it. That's the entire staff requirement.

What perk should I offer?

Whatever works for your margins. Popular options: free side with purchase (restaurants), 10% off (retail), free entry before 10pm (bars/lounges), free small upgrade (hookah). Not sure? Reach out and we'll suggest one.

What counts as a "wheel prize donation"?

Any product, gift card, or credit worth $10+. We add a slot on the spin wheel with your name. Players see it every time they spin — all night.

Is this really just one day?

Yes. July 11, 4–10pm. Concentrated intentionally — one big activation creates urgency, competition, and corridor-wide energy.

Is the app actually built?

Yes. Live at ustreet-hunt.atcheofficial.com. Supabase backend running. Cookies DC is anchor partner. The event happens — the question is whether you're on the map.
