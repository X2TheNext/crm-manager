Key Budget Assumptions & Tips

  • Prize Pool Offset: Leverage Featured Partners ($250 tier) to sponsor the grand prize — a VIP table or large gift card — in exchange for their logo on the passport. This drops your out-of-pocket prize cost significantly.
  • Passport Quality: Don't skimp on paper. People carry these in pockets and bags for a week (July 11–18). Heavy cardstock only — it needs to feel premium and survive.
  • Self-Inking Stamps Only: A busy bartender won't deal with a separate wooden stamp and ink pad. Self-inking keeps it frictionless for venue staff — no mess, no lost parts.
  • Print Deadlines: Custom stamps and specialty cardstock booklets need 7–10 day turnaround. Order immediately to avoid rush shipping fees (which add 20–30% to your print costs).
